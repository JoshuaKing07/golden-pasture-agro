Our Passion for Livestock

Golden Pasture Agro isn't just a farm; it's a commitment to quality. While others focus on crops, we have mastered the art of animal husbandry. Our fishery utilizes modern water-recirculation systems, and our livestock are raised in open-space pastures to ensure they are healthy and strong.

Our Mission

To provide healthy, organic protein sources to our community.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of exotic livestock in the region.

Sustainability: Protecting the land for the next generation.
Integrity: Honest pricing and healthy animals, always.
Quality: We only sell what we would feed our own families.
